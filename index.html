Texas Blockchain

Who we are

A non-profit foundation ran by students at The University of Texas, an overseeing advisory board consisting of industry professionals, and corporate sponsors ranging from startups to Fortune 500 companies. We are dedicated to serving the local Austin blockchain communities by hosting events, incubating startups, and distributing resources. Our members consist of undergraduates, graduates, alumni, professors, and foundation members.

Student Membership

Texas Blockchain's student membership consists of education, research, development, and more. Collaboration is crucial amongst students for UT to lead the way in distributed ledger technology. Since blockchain technology is such a multi-disciplinary industry we encourage all UT students wanting to get involved on campus to apply.

Foundation Membership

Texas Blockchain Foundation is always seeking new members in order to better the blockchain community at The University of Texas as well as in Austin. Membership is open to all individuals or organizations across the world interested in giving back to student start ups, collaborating with students, or innovating amongst students.

Our Mission

Texas Blockchain aims to empower students to learn about and develop distributed technology solutions and acquire valuable skills through education, research projects, and working hands on with the community. Premised on the idea that universities are a hub for visionary ideas and cutting-edge thoughts, Texas Blockchain has set out to bring together an extraordinary group of individuals from the University of Texas at Austin to learn, discuss and develop what we see as one of the most disruptive technologies of the future. By positioning ourselves as the bridge between academia and industry, Texas Blockchain will allow students and large players in the corporate sector to come together and develop the next transformative, cutting-edge ideas of the 21st century.

Education

We teach students blockchain technology through guided coursework, organize meetups, and host tech talks, discussions, and panels. Our goal of education is to foster proper adoption of blockchain technology in the Austin area.

Research and Development

We build projects and work alongside professors to create groundbreaking innovations in the blockchain space. All of our work is open source.

Consultancy

We work hands on with companies to guide and develop implementation for blockchain technology. We build proof of concepts, roadmaps, and starting points for companies to use for future their products.

Our Incubator at the Blackstone Launchpad

Located at the Flawn Academic Center, our inucator aims to provide blockchain based startups with the necessary tools they need to grow and succeed. Texas Blockchain Foundation aims to be the hub of blockchain and cryptoeconomic entrepreneurship for the Austin community. We expect to do this by creating a network of multi-disciplinary professionals that all collaborate to become innovators in the blockchain space.

If you have a startup idea and are interested in being incubated by Texas Blockchain, please fill out the application request form below. We will review your submission promptly.
