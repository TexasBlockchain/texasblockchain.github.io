Who We Are

Texas Blockchain aims to empower students to learn and develop distributed technology solutions while acquiring valuable skills through education, research projects, and discussion. Premised on the idea that universities are a hub for visionary ideas and cutting-edge thoughts, Texas Blockchain has set out to bring together an extraordinary group of individuals from the University of Texas at Austin to learn, discuss and develop what we see as one of the disruptive technologies of the future. By positioning ourself as the bridge between academia and industry, Texas Blockchain will allow students and large players in the corporate sector to come together and develop the next transformative, cutting-edge ideas of the 21st century.
